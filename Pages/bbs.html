BBS

"BBS" stands for "Be Back Soon." It is often used to indicate that the person will be away or temporarily unavailable but plans to return shortly.

Example Usage:

"I need to step out for a moment, so I'll put up a quick BBS sign on my desk."

"Going to grab a coffee, BBS!"

"Leaving my desk for a short break, BBS."
